CareSwap CareForum, CareMotivate & Admin Page

Project Goal & Information: The goal was to build a positive, family-friendly community platform web application for DellInnovateFest Competition 2026 to reduce the generational gap between youths and seniors, by creating a digital community space with many features with accessible interfaces so users can use it regardless of their digital proficiency levels.

My Role and Solution: My scope covered creating a moderated digital space where youths and seniors could communicate safely. I referenced community platforms like Reddit and Instagram to shape usable forum and interaction flows.

My Work Process: I developed CareForum and CareMotivate features such as posting, commenting, categorisation, motivational elements, and admin moderation workflows. I contributed to key pages and interactions using Flask, Jinja, HTML/CSS, Tailwind, JavaScript, Gemini API, and Firebase while keeping navigation clear and content handling scalable.

Results Achieved: The final solution delivered clear posting and moderation workflows, improved participation across users, and supported stronger youth-senior bonds through a friendly community space.

Nanyang Polytechnic E-Sports CCA Page

Project Goal & Information: The goal for the project was to build a clear and engaging website for NYP E-Sports CCA that presents its mission, history, games and achievements for members and visitors, in a bid to encourage more members to join the CCA. I worked with my Polytechnic classmate for this project.

My Role and Solution: My role was to plan and develop key content pages that improved the CCA's online presence, and to provide a platform for prospective members to learn more about the CCA. I focused on designing readable, user-friendly layouts and clear content flows so users could easily understand the information.

My Work Process: I created the About page, game-related pages to document awards, and a credits page to attribute media used. I implemented the frontend using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript while maintaining visual consistency across pages.

Results Achieved: The final website was a clean and informative platform that improved content accessibility and helped users understand the CCA's identity and accomplishments, while incentivizing prospective members to join the CCA.
